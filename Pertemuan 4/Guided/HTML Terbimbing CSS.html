Synopsis

The series will revolve around Park Hoon, a South Korean who was captured with his fateher and grows up in North Korea. He is trained to become a genius doctor, and through Park Hoon lands a job at the best South Korean hospital after defecting, he is treated as foreigner To find his lost North Korean first love, Song Jae Hee, he does anythong to make money.
